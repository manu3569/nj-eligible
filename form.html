How many W-2 full time equivalent (FTE) employees does your business have? Please include yourself as one employee. If you have part time employees, for each part-time W-2 employee estimate the number of hours worked per week, divide that number by 35 and that is the factional FTE estimate for that employee. Sum all fractional FTEs for part-time W-2 employees and add that to 1 FTE for each full-time employee. Do not include 1099 employees in this calculation

Ineligible Business

If your business or non-profit is not registered to do business in New Jersey, or is an ineligible business type, NJEDA cannot assist.

Consider these two sources of private financing:

  • Loans from a CDFI
  • Financing from a private bank
